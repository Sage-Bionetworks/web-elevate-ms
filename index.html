Living with MS is an ever-changing story. MS can bring on symptoms and relapses unexpectedly. Some days are easy, while on other days your MS symptoms consume your story.

Help Us Understand

No two people are alike when it comes to living with Multiple Sclerosis (MS). The elevate MS study is an effort to better understand what may trigger flare up of MS symptoms? In research, every experience counts. Whether you have MS, are touched by someone who has or has had MS, or you simply want to help, we invite you to participate in this study and help us understand MS.

Telling Your Story

Life with MS isn’t a one size fits all. Everyone has unique experiences even though there are common symptoms. elevateMS is a tool for you to understand your MS symptoms and the factors that impact you. Reflecting on your story over time may provide you with insights into your MS Story and your overall wellness.
