About the Study

The elevateMS study, developed in collaboration by Sage Bionetworks (nonprofit) and Novartis Pharmaceuticals Corporation, monitors wellbeing and changes in selected symptoms of MS such as dexterity, balance or gait. The app lets you record your own story of living with or without active symptoms and allows you to securely share information with researchers. Sage Bionetworks developed the free elevateMS app to be a research tool that may also help you track your symptoms whenever you want. It is about you in real life. You are in charge.

So long as you are 18 years old or older, live in the U.S. and have a personal iPhone (model 5 or newer with iOS 8 or later), then you can join elevateMS. You do not need to have MS to join this study and make a difference in the lives of people affected by MS.

How the Study Works

Step 1

Download the App

In the study we will monitor your wellbeing and changes in selected symptoms of MS. We will ask you to download the elevateMS app.

Step 2

Consent

To get started, we will ask you to do an informed consent process. It takes about 20 minutes. We will also ask you to do a one-time health survey. It takes about 20 minutes.

Step 3

Perform simple tasks

You will receive notices on your phone asking you to complete simple weekly activities. These activities may be walking with your phone in the pocket or gently tapping the screen with your fingers. There will also be brain tests. These activities should take you about 10 minutes each week. You decide how many and when to do these activities but we’d love if you could do these once a week.

From time to time you will receive short surveys. Surveys may be about your health, exercise, diet, sleep and medicines. You can skip any question you do not want to answer. We may send you the same surveys multiple times or ask you if you notice any changes in your health.

Step 4

Track your health

You can view past activities on the app’s dashboard. As you use the app you may see important changes and trends. This may help you recognize why you experience certain symptoms, and how your symptoms change over time.

Step 5

Scientists make discoveries

When you use the app you contribute to research on MS. By combining the information from many people who are using the app, researchers expect to find patterns. The information you provide through the elevateMS app gets encrypted on the phone to protect your privacy. As you participate over time, you help create a powerful dataset that may lead to scientific breakthrough and to new ways to help people with MS improve their well being. You do not need to have MS to participate. This study will last about one year. We’d like you to participate for three consecutive months or longer if you can.
