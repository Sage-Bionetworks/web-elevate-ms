Understand your MS

No two people are alike when it comes to living with MS. The elevate MS study is an effort to better understand what may trigger flare up of MS symptoms. In research, every experience counts. Whether you have MS, are touched by someone who has or has had MS, or you simply want to help, we invite you to participate in this study.

Telling Your Story

Life with MS isn’t a one size fits all. Everyone has unique experiences even though there are common symptoms.

elevateMS is a tool to understand your MS triggers, specific MS symptoms and your overall wellness. It is about you in real life.

If you are 18 or older, live in the US, and have a personal iPhone, please consider joining this study. You do not need to have MS to join this study and make a difference in the lives of people affected by MS.
